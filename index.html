My personal journey through fitness and wellbeing:

For the past five years of my life, I have dedicated every moment I have free to devote it to improving my fitness, personal health and strength within the gym. I find that it is one of the most rewarding and important things to do, even if that is devoting 45 minutes a day in order to keep yourself feeling good.
Furthermore, the reason I feel that fitness is important to anybody, no matter how young or old, is that you can have another goal to look forward to beating, setting personal challenges within the gym is always rewarding, and the journey you go through whilst doing so can improve your mood- and mental health.

My Love for Fitness:

My fitness journey initially started because I never had the self confidence to speak to people, and I was extremely introverted when I was in secondary school, and throughout the years after.

The second I set foot in the gym, instantly I knew it would be an extreme challenge for me, and because of that, my dedication slowly started to build. Many years down the line after I had went to the gym as consistently as I possibly could, something clicked in my head and at that moment, I decided to dedicate as much time as I had in my free time to bettering myself physically, and mentally.
