My personal journey through fitness and wellbeing:

For the past five years of my life, I have dedicated every moment I have free to devote it to improving my fitness, personal health and strength within the gym. I find that it is one of the most rewarding and important things to do, even if that is devoting 45 minutes a day in order to keep yourself feeling good.

My Love for Fitness:

My fitness journey initially started because I never had the self confidence to speak to people, and I was extremely introverted when I was in secondary school, and throughout the years after. The second I set foot in the gym, instantly I knew it would be an extreme challenge for me, and because of that, my dedication slowly started to build

Many years down the line after I had went to the gym as consistently as I possibly could, something clicked in my head and at that moment, I decided to dedicate as much time as I had in my free time to bettering myself physically, and mentally.
